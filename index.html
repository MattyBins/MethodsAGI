More than 80% of the digital world is informing themselves via some form of AI or Search Generative Experience, such as the AI and algorithms found in Google and ChatGPT. How can we learn what they're learning? MethodsAGI enables researchers to simulate and analyze the AI and algorithms found in these everyday learning systems. Using simple A/B test simulations, MethodsAGI streamlines the process of collecting user interactions, highlighting qualitative differences, calculating statistical differences, and then visualizing and reporting the analysis.
